---
title: "Can you illuminate a Book of Hours?"
description: "A historical game in which players run a 13th-century Parisian atelier producing an illuminated manuscript for a noble patron — managing parchment, pigment, gold leaf, and time."
---

The year is 1265. In a small Parisian atelier, a master illuminator and her apprentices begin work on a Book of Hours for a noble patron. Parchment must be sourced and prepared, pigments ground, gold leaf laid, and miniatures designed and painted. The deadline is fixed; the patron's expectations are exacting; the budget — 125 livres — has to stretch across every choice. Time, coin, and craft are all you have.
